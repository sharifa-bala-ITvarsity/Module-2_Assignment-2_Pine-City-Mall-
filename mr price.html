MR PRICE

Mr. Price is a South African value fashion retailer, selling mainly own-brand products. The company is one of the nation's largest clothing retailers.

We're bringing hot fashion across ladies, men and kids at unbelievable value to stores across Africa, and worldwide through mrp.com and the Mr Price app.

Mr Price Group is a cash-based fashion-value retailer, and is a public company listed on the Johannesburg Stock Exchange. Established in 1985, the retailer has 2,543 stores which are mainly in South Africa, as well as online channels.
